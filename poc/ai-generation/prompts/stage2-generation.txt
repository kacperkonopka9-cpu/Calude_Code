### SEKCJA 2: OPIS PROJEKTU (Project Description)

**Purpose:** Comprehensive technical description of what will be developed and how.

**Content Requirements:**
- Detailed description of the R&D work to be performed
- Technologies, methods, and approaches to be used
- Scope of work (what's included, what's excluded)
- Expected deliverables (prototypes, documentation, etc.)

**Length:** 800-1000 words (3-5 paragraphs)

**Critical:** Must be technical, not marketing language. Focus on "what" and "how," not "why it's great."

### SEKCJA 3: NOWATORSKOŚĆ I INNOWACYJNOŚĆ (Innovation and Novelty)

**Purpose:** Explain what makes this project innovative and novel.

**Content Requirements:**
- What is new compared to existing solutions in the market/organization?
- State-of-the-art analysis (current solutions and their limitations)
- How this project advances beyond current state-of-the-art
- Level of innovation (incremental vs. breakthrough)
- Competitive advantage or technical leap

**Length:** 600-800 words (3-4 paragraphs)

**Critical:** Must explicitly reference ELEMENT TWÓRCZY (creative element) and NOWA WIEDZA (new knowledge).

### SEKCJA 4: METODOLOGIA PRAC B+R (R&D Methodology)

**Purpose:** Describe the systematic R&D approach and methods used.

**Length:** 600-800 words (3-4 paragraphs)

**Critical:** Must demonstrate systematic, planned approach (not trial-and-error or ad-hoc).

### SEKCJA 5: PROBLEMY BADAWCZE (Research Problems)

**Purpose:** Identify and elaborate on technical problems requiring R&D investigation.

**Content Requirements:**
- List 4-6 specific technical challenges

- Interdependencies between problems

**Length:** 800-1000 words (4-6 mini-sections, one per problem)

**Critical:** These must be genuine technical uncertainties, not routine engineering problems.
